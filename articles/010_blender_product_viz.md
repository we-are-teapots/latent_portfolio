# Japanese Kyusu Side-Handle Teapot

An authentic Japanese Kyusu teapot crafted in the Tokoname tradition, featuring a characteristic side handle and integrated ceramic filter. Hand-formed from local red clay rich in iron, this teapot is specifically designed for brewing premium Japanese green teas, with the clay's natural properties enhancing flavor profiles and water softness.
