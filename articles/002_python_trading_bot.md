# Wheel-Thrown Stoneware Teapot Series

A collection of functional stoneware teapots meticulously crafted on the potter's wheel, each piece demonstrating mastery of throwing techniques, handle attachment, and spout precision. The series features varied capacities from 400ml to 800ml, finished with food-safe glazes and tested for optimal pour and balance.
